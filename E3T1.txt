# Research the AWS Guard Duty documentation page and explain how GuardDuty may have detected this attack - i.e. what was its source of information.
From document: https://docs.aws.amazon.com/guardduty/latest/ug/guardduty_findings.html
To detect unauthorized and unexpected activity in AWS environment. There are two primary details that determine what kind of information is available for any finding. The first is the resource type, which can be Instance, AccessKey, S3Bucket, S3Object, Kubernetes cluster, ECS cluster, Container, RDSDBInstance, or Lambda. The second detail that determines finding information is Resource Role. Resource role can be Target for access keys, meaning the resource was the target of suspicious activity.
Full Information about individual activity attempts will still be available in your CloudTrail or VPC Flow Logs.
